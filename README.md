# Business-risk-and-marketing-management-sytem
📌 Project Overview

The Integrated Business Risk & Marketing Management System is a data analytics project designed to help  businesses monitor financial performance, customer behavior, marketing effectiveness, and operational risks through a centralized analytical framework.

This project combines sales analytics, customer segmentation, churn analysis, marketing ROI evaluation, and business risk assessment into a single system to support data-driven decision-making and strategic planning.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze sales trends and profitability
Perform customer segmentation using RFM analysis
Identify customer churn and revenue loss
Evaluate marketing campaign performance
Develop a Business Risk Scoring Model
Create interactive dashboards for business insights
